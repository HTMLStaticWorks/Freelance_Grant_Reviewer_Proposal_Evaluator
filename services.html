Our Process

How the Review Works

1

Submission & Intake

Upload your proposal through the secure client portal. Complete the intake form specifying the funder, deadline, grant amount, and any areas of concern. We review your submission within 24 hours and confirm your assignment.

2

Funder Research

Before reading your proposal, we research your target funder—their stated priorities, past funded projects, scoring rubrics, and reviewer guidelines. This ensures our feedback reflects what will actually matter to the decision-makers.

3

Deep Evaluation

We read your proposal multiple times, scoring each section against the funder's criteria and adding inline annotations. We identify both strengths to preserve and weaknesses to address.

4

Report Writing

We compile findings into a structured PDF evaluation report with executive summary, section-by-section scores, detailed narrative feedback, and a prioritized revision checklist.

5

Delivery & Follow-Up

Your report is uploaded to the client portal for secure download. We follow up 48 hours later to answer any questions and, for Standard/Premium plans, schedule your consultation call.
