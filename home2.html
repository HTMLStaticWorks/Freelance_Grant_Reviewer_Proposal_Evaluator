FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before submitting your proposal.

How long does a review take?

Standard reviews are delivered within 5 business days. Premium plan clients receive priority 3-day turnaround. Rush 48-hour reviews are available upon request for an additional fee.

What file formats do you accept?

We accept PDF, Word (.docx), and Google Docs submissions. Files up to 50MB are supported through our secure client portal. All uploads are encrypted in transit and at rest.

Is my proposal kept confidential?

Absolutely. All reviewers sign strict NDAs and your documents are never shared, published, or used as samples without explicit written consent. We take confidentiality extremely seriously.

What types of grants do you review?

We review federal, state, private foundation, and corporate grants across all nonprofit sectors including education, health, housing, workforce, arts, environment, and social services.

Do you guarantee funding success?

No reviewer can guarantee funding—that decision rests with the funder. However, we guarantee the quality of our review: if you're not satisfied, we'll revise the evaluation at no charge.
